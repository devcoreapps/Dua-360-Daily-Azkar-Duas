Privacy Policy for Dua 360 - Daily Azkar Duas

Dua 360 - Daily Azkar Duas is your daily companion for authentic duas and adhkar. This Privacy Policy explains how the App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use it. We built the App to be usable fully offline with no account at all — anything beyond that is optional and explained below.

Quick Summary

  • The App works completely offline. You are never required to create an account or sign in.
  • Without signing in, your bookmarks and read history are stored only on your device and are never sent to us.
  • Signing in with Google is entirely optional and exists for one purpose: backing up your bookmarks and read history to the cloud so they survive a reinstall or lost device, and so they sync across your devices.
  • We do not show ads, run analytics or tracking SDKs, or sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

1Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Only on Your Device (No Account Required)

When you use the App without signing in, the following is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Bookmarked duas — which duas you have saved
  • Read history — which duas you have marked as read, used to show your reading progress
  • App preferences — display language, translation language, theme (light/dark), font size and font choice, and notification reminder times

This data stays on your device. Uninstalling the App or clearing its data removes it permanently, and we have no copy of it.

1.2 Information Collected When You Sign In (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google to enable backup and sync, we collect and store the following in our cloud database (Google Cloud Firestore), associated with your account:

  • Basic profile information from your Google account: email address, display name, and profile photo URL
  • Your bookmarks, including which dua was bookmarked, the date and time it was bookmarked, its category, and the language you were using
  • Your read history, including which duas you marked as read and when

This is the same data described in section 1.1 — signing in simply copies it to the cloud (and back down to any other device where you sign in) so it isn't lost. We do not collect any additional personal information through the sign-in process beyond what Google provides for authentication.

1.3 Push Notification Token

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to support push notifications. This generates a unique device token used solely to deliver notifications to your device. This token does not contain personal information and is not linked to your identity unless you are also signed in.

1.4 Information We Do Not Collect

No analytics SDKs No advertising SDKs No crash reporting No location tracking No contacts access No ads displayed

We do not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs. We do not collect your location, contacts, browsing history, or any device identifiers beyond the push notification token described above. We do not display ads.

2How We Use Your Information

Information collected (whether stored locally or, if you sign in, in the cloud) is used only to:

  • Display your bookmarked and recently read duas
  • Track and display your reading progress
  • Restore your bookmarks and read history when you sign in on a new or reinstalled copy of the App
  • Apply your saved preferences (language, theme, font size, reminder times)
  • Deliver the daily reminder notifications you've enabled

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the functioning of the App.

3Data Storage and Security

  • Locally stored data lives in the App's private storage area on your device, governed by your device's own security model.
  • Cloud-stored data (for signed-in users) is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, secured by Google's infrastructure and access-controlled so that only your authenticated account can read or write your own data.
  • We do not maintain our own servers that store your personal data outside of Firebase/Google Cloud's managed infrastructure.
Note: No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable, industry-standard measures to protect your information.

4Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We use the following third-party services to operate the App, each of which processes data on our behalf as described above:

Service Purpose Data Involved
Firebase Authentication / Google Sign-In Optional sign-in Email, display name, profile photo
Cloud Firestore Optional cloud backup of bookmarks/read history Bookmarks, read history, basic profile fields
Firebase Cloud Messaging Push notification delivery Device notification token

These providers (Google/Firebase) may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not share your data with any other third party.

5Your Rights and Choices

  • Use the App without an account. Signing in is never required.
  • Sign out at any time (Settings → Sign Out). Your bookmarks and read history remain safely on your device — signing out does not delete anything locally.
  • Delete your account and cloud data (Settings → Delete Account). This permanently deletes your bookmarks, read history, and profile information from our cloud database across all devices, and cannot be undone. Data already stored locally on a given device is separate and is removed by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage.
  • Control notifications at any time from Settings, including disabling reminders entirely.

6Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is intended for general audiences interested in Islamic supplications (duas), and account sign-in is optional and not required for any core feature.
